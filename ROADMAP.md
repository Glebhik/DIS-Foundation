# Roadmap — DIS Group

This document translates the [VISION.md](VISION.md) into a sequenced, prioritized plan. It defines what DIS Group is building now, what comes next, and what we are explicitly deferring.

This is a living document. It is updated quarterly by the CEO and Strategy Lead.

---

## Roadmap Philosophy

We do not plan in detail beyond 12 months — conditions change too fast. Instead:

- **Now (0–12 months):** Detailed, committed, owned
- **Next (12–36 months):** Directional, capacity-planned, reviewed quarterly
- **Later (36 months+):** Strategic intent only — preserved for context, not execution

Every item on this roadmap links to the domain where it is being executed.

## What We Are Not Doing Now

Deliberate deferrals help teams focus. These items are acknowledged but explicitly deprioritized until Phase 1 is complete:

| Item | Reason Deferred | Revisit |
|---|---|---|
| International expansion | Operating system must be solid before geographic scaling | End of 2026 |
| External product/platform launch | Internal capability must be proven first | 2027+ |
| M&A activity | Integration capacity not yet built | 2027+ |
| Public reporting / PR campaigns | Need substance before narrative | 2027 |
