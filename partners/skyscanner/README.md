## Partnership Thesis

Skyscanner holds the world's most comprehensive real-time travel content. DIS Travel is an AI-native Travel Intelligence Platform for corporate buyers. The partnership is complementary — Skyscanner serves travellers; DIS Travel serves the organisations that employ them.

The integration architecture is designed around three components:
1. **Content Foundation** — Skyscanner travel content as DIS Travel's data layer
2. **Booking Distribution** — Enterprise bookings routed through Skyscanner's supplier ecosystem
3. **Carbon Data Collaboration** — Route-level aircraft and carrier data enabling accurate Scope 3 carbon calculation
