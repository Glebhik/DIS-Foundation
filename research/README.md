# Research

Market research, industry analysis, competitive intelligence, and internal studies.

## Contents

| File / Folder | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `market/` | Market sizing and trend reports |
| `competitive/` | Competitor profiles and win/loss analysis |
| `user-research/` | User interviews, surveys, usability studies |
| `industry/` | Industry reports and news digests |
| `internal/` | Internal performance studies and retrospectives |

## Guidelines

- Tag each document with `[DATE]`, `[SOURCE]`, and `[CONFIDENCE: High/Medium/Low]`.
- Research older than 18 months should be reviewed and either updated or archived.
