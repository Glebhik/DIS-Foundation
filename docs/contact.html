Partnership Enquiries

For the best response

Partnership conversations move faster when the initial message includes context about your organisation and the specific opportunity. The following helps us respond with the right information and the right person:

  • Your organisation and your role
  • Which DIS Group product or business unit is relevant
  • The specific capability or data type you are interested in discussing
  • Whether this is an initial exploration or a specific technical integration proposal

We do not respond to unsolicited sales outreach. We respond to every substantive partnership or technical enquiry.

For DIS Travel Partnerships

First conversation

The initial ask for a DIS Travel partnership discussion is a 45-minute conversation with a DIS Group technical or partnerships contact.

Before that conversation, DIS Group can share:

  • Full Technical Overview (architecture, data model, integration design)
  • Executive One-Pager (development stage, capabilities, partnership thesis)
  • Team Profile (leadership, operating history, governance framework)
  • AI Governance Policy (Class 1–4 framework, pre-deployment review process)

All documents are available upon request — no NDA required for initial discussion materials.
