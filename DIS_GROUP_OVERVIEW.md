# DIS Group — Company Overview

DIS Group is a diversified industrial and technology company operating across recycling, energy, and digital infrastructure. The group is built on the conviction that the most valuable businesses of the next decade sit at the intersection of physical-world industrial operations and intelligent software systems.

---

## Mission

To build resilient, technology-driven businesses that create long-term value by solving real problems in energy, materials, and information.

---

## Business Portfolio

DIS Group currently operates across three core business units, supported by shared corporate functions and a unified technology and AI capability layer.

### Recycling

The recycling business handles the collection, processing, and commercial distribution of recyclable materials. Operations are built around efficiency, regulatory compliance, and commodity market intelligence.

**Core activities:** Materials intake and sorting, processing and quality grading, offtake sales, environmental compliance
**Strategic position:** Building scalable, technology-augmented processing capacity with real-time commodity pricing intelligence
**Key documents:** [recycling/overview.md](recycling/README.md) | [recycling/kpis.md](recycling/README.md)

---

### Energy

The energy business develops, operates, and trades energy assets. The portfolio may span generation, storage, and supply depending on market conditions and regulatory opportunity.

**Core activities:** Asset development and management, energy trading, regulatory engagement, project origination
**Strategic position:** Building a differentiated position through asset selectivity and trading intelligence
**Key documents:** [energy/overview.md](energy/README.md) | [energy/assets/](energy/README.md)

---

### ESG Platform

DIS Group treats ESG not as a reporting obligation but as a strategic capability. The ESG platform integrates environmental, social, and governance data across all business units to drive better decisions, access capital markets, and meet stakeholder expectations.

**Core activities:** Carbon accounting, social impact measurement, governance frameworks, external reporting
**Strategic position:** Best-in-class data infrastructure for ESG, enabling credible commitments and verified reporting
**Key documents:** [esg/framework.md](esg/README.md) | [esg/targets.md](esg/README.md)

## How the Businesses Connect

DIS Group's diversification is intentional but not arbitrary. The businesses share:

1. **A capital allocation model** — Finance evaluates opportunities consistently across units using shared return frameworks
2. **A technology backbone** — All units benefit from shared infrastructure, data infrastructure, and AI capabilities
3. **An ESG platform** — Environmental and governance data flows from operations into a unified reporting layer
4. **A research function** — Market intelligence is shared across units to identify intersections and avoid blind spots
