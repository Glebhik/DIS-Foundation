# Travel Intelligence

DIS Group's Travel Intelligence domain covers two distinct areas: the **DIS Travel product** — an AI-powered Travel Intelligence Platform for corporate buyers — and the group's own internal corporate travel management.

---

## DIS Travel — The Product

DIS Travel is DIS Group's external product: an AI-native Travel Intelligence Platform designed for organisations that need to make better decisions about how, where, and why their people travel.

**Core intelligence capabilities:**
- Travel policy intelligence and real-time compliance monitoring
- Destination intelligence — risk, visa, local context, synthesised by AI
- Carbon and ESG tracking — Scope 3 business travel emissions, route-level attribution
- Market intelligence — pricing patterns, booking window optimisation, supplier performance
- Supplier analytics — performance tracking across carriers, hotels, and ground transport

**Status:** Foundation phase — architecture designed, implementation in progress.
